About the Project

In the modern advertising landscape, digital signage is a powerful tool for delivering personalized advertisements. Our system uses AI to analyze data, gain insights, and deliver engaging content based on consumer demographics, behavior, and preferences. By leveraging Computer Vision and Large Language Models (LLMs), we create a real-time interactive and adaptive advertisement experience.

Key Features

Targeted Advertising Delivery

Our system analyzes audience data in real-time and delivers advertisements based on their relevance to the audience profile and location context.

Interactive Feedback System

Digital signage allows audience interaction through touch screens and other devices, enabling more personalized experiences and gathering valuable feedback.

Analytics & Optimization

Detailed analytics on campaign performance helps optimize future advertisements and fine-tune targeting, messaging, and timing strategies.

Tasks & Deliverables

  • Develop and train computer vision models for demographics estimation
  • Integrate LLM for personalized content generation
  • Build user-friendly advertising campaign management interface
  • Implement seamless data pipelines for data collection, processing, and integration
  • Conduct rigorous testing and validation of AI models
  • Deliver a working prototype with technical documentation
